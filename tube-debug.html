Tube debug

Hits each endpoint directly and shows exactly what TfL returned. Nothing here touches the app.

1 · Line status

Every line, its status and severity. ?debug=1 shows the raw TfL payload.

—

2 · Station search

Find a station's TfL id (you need these ids for the planner below).

—

3 · Journey planner

The important one. Returns the legs, and — crucially — the resolved direction per leg. Use Directions to see exactly how each direction was worked out.

—
Defaults are Waterloo → Canary Wharf. Grab other ids from the search above. A 300 from TfL means "which station did you mean?" — hit Disambiguation to see the candidates and which one we picked. Ids starting HUB are the usual culprit.

4 · Arrivals for a leg

Direction-filtered live trains. after = minutes until you'd actually reach that platform (0 for the first leg). Raw shows everything TfL sent, including the wrong-way trains that were dropped.

—
If wrongWayDropped is 0 and you expected trains both ways, the direction filter may not be working.

5 · Station crowding

Live busyness, as a fraction of the busiest that station has ever been (TfL recalculates every ~5 min). A few stations have no data at all.

—
If available:false, TfL has no crowding data for that station — we show nothing rather than guess.

6 · Follow a bus

Where a specific vehicle is on its route. Get a vehicle registration from /api/busboard?stop=… first. Raw shows TfL's per-vehicle predictions and the route sequence.

—
A stop counts as passed when it drops out of TfL's prediction list for that vehicle — real tracking, not a timetable comparison.

7 · Historic reliability (HSP)

A train's track record over the past ~3 months. Set HSP_KEY (the RDM consumer key) and HSP_URL (endpoint base from the product's Specification tab) in Cloudflare, Production + Preview. Legacy HSP_USER/HSP_PASS also still works. Raw shows the route used and exactly what HSP returned.

—
If available:false, the reason field says why (key missing, URL missing, auth failed, or timed out). sample is how many past trains the percentages are based on.

8 · National buses (BODS)

Live bus positions outside London. Needs BODS_KEY in Cloudflare (free from data.bus-data.dft.gov.uk, Account → API key). Raw shows a sample of the SIRI XML.

—
Default coords are Banstead (outside TfL's area). available:false with a reason means key/config; an empty vehicles list just means nothing is reporting there right now.
